DepositAccount

A Schema.org Type
Thing > Intangible > Service > FinancialProduct > BankAccount > DepositAccount
Thing > Intangible > Service > FinancialProduct > InvestmentOrDeposit > DepositAccount
  • Canonical URL: http://schema.org/DepositAccount
  • Leave public feedback on this term 💬

A type of Bank Account with a main purpose of depositing funds to gain interest or other benefits.
