RadioBroadcastService

A Schema.org Type

This term is proposed for full integration into Schema.org, pending implementation feedback and adoption from applications and websites.
Thing > Intangible > Service > BroadcastService > RadioBroadcastService
  • Canonical URL: http://schema.org/RadioBroadcastService
  • Leave public feedback on this term 💬

A delivery service through which radio content is provided via broadcast over the air or online.
